Help

This combines all the listed google calendar id and generates a link to view the combined calendar. This is useful for scheduling meeting amongs multiple parties.

Pre requisite

  1. Google Calendar reflects your free time accurately.
  2. You will need to make your calendar public (can restrict to show only free/busy time)

Enter google ID manually

  1. Choose either single insertion mode or multiple insertion mode
  2. Click on link or copy link text

Import from google manually

  1. Click on import from Google button
  2. Give permission (The least permission needed, just need to view your calendar for it's ID)
  3. Filter the list of calendar by removing calendars that are not relevant
